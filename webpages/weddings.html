Weddings

At Belladonna, we understand the magic of planning your dream wedding. With 8 years experience and limitless passion for flowers, we love turning your dreams into a reality. Whether you're looking for something simple and sophisticated, wild and natural, or something completely off the wall, at Belladonna we will always put your needs first and strive to complete your perfect day. We use only the highest quality Dutch and local flowers and foliages, as well as the best quality ribbons, glassware and accessories. We then combine this with your ideas and any images that inspire you, to create a wedding collection completely unique to you, at a price to suit your budget.
